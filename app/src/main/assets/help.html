Mausam

Weather, everybody wants to know how it is going to be during the week. Will it be rainy, windy, or sunny? Luckily for us, in the information age, there are open APIs to retrieve information and Mausam will help to keep things handy and it will keep you updated.

● Home screen:

    ○ Showing a list of locations that the user has bookmarked previously.

    ○ To remove locations from the list, Kindly swipe the item to left side a confirmation dialog will appear to acknowledge the same

    ○ Add locations by placing a pin on map. After placing pin please click on bookmark button on right top corner.

● City screen: once the user clicks on a bookmarked city this screen will appear on home screen.

    ○ On click of the city card weather data is displayed on new screen.

    ○ Forecast such as temperature, humidity, rain chances and wind information are displayed along with the next 5 days forecast

● Settings screen:

    ○ You can choose the unit type among Standard, Metric and Imperial to view the weather.

    ○ You can clear all the saved cities by clicking on option available on screen.
