Mausam

Weather, everybody wants to know how it is going to be during the week. Will it be rainy, windy, or sunny? Luckily for us, in the information age, there are open APIs to retrieve information and Mausam will help to keep things handy and it will keep you updated.

● Home screen:

    ○ Showing a list of locations that the user has bookmarked previously.

    ○ Show a way to remove locations from the list.

    ○ Add locations by placing a pin on map.

● City screen: once the user clicks on a bookmarked city this screen will appear

    ○ Today’s forecast such as temperature, humidity, rain chances and wind information.
